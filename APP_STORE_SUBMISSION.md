**Description**

Learn something worthwhile in the time it takes to make a cup of tea.

New in 5 gives you one calm, focused lesson at a time. Choose history, science, space, nature or evidence-based outlooks about the future — or let the app surprise you.

Each lesson is split into three simple cards, followed by one quick question to help the idea stick. Switch between Simple and Standard reading modes whenever you like.

WHY NEW IN 5?

• Short lessons designed for a genuine five-minute break  
• 126 ideas across five varied subjects  
• Named sources and review information for every lesson

• Simple and Standard reading modes for different readers  
• Save interesting lessons on your device  
• No streak pressure, endless feed, account, advertising or tracking

For curious children, adults and families who want to leave a spare moment knowing something new.

## App privacy answers

- Select **Data Not Collected**.
- There are no accounts, analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs or payments in version 1.0.
- Saved and seen lesson identifiers are stored only on the device using local storage.
- Recheck these answers before any future release that adds analytics, accounts, purchases, cloud sync or crash-reporting services.

## Age rating and content

Answer the questionnaire from the actual app content. For version 1.0:

- No user-generated content inside the app.
- No messaging or chat.
- No advertising.
- No gambling, sexual content, profanity, graphic violence, medical treatment or substance-use content.
- Curated source links open external websites; they are not an unrestricted in-app web browser.
- Do not mark the app as Made for Kids.

Apple calculates the final regional age ratings from these answers.

## Phone and tablet test checklist

- [ ] Fresh install opens to a complete card with no scrolling.
- [ ] Every subject chip selects an appropriate lesson.
- [ ] Surprise shuffles across subjects and avoids immediate repeats.
- [ ] Swipe and button navigation both work.
- [ ] All three lesson cards fit the display.
- [ ] The quick question can be answered and completed.
- [ ] Simple and Standard modes both fit.
- [ ] Saving and reopening a lesson works.
- [ ] Source links, fact reporting, support and privacy links open correctly.
- [ ] Airplane mode still allows bundled lessons to be read; external links fail gracefully outside the app.
- [ ] VoiceOver can identify navigation, save, reading-mode, back, source and answer controls.
- [ ] Larger text and landscape orientation do not hide required controls.
- [ ] Relaunching the app preserves saved/seen progress locally.
